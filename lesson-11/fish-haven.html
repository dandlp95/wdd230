Swimming is still an option!

By Daniel De La Pena

It is no secret that Idaho is one of the coldest places in the region, and with Fish Haven, this is is especially true. Because of this, a lot of locals often get sad that swimming is not an option. This is true if we are talking about lakes and rivers, but do not forget that Fish Haven is a place rich in in inside public pools.

Most of the locals do a great job at keeping the public pool in awesome condition. They are also extremely sanatary. Do keep in mind that all of them keep safety seriously, so if you want to bring any your kids to swim, they have to come with someone 18 or older, otherwise they will not be allowed to enter due to safety issues which is understable because there are no a lot of lifeguards.
