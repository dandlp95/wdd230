Description

Our audience will be busy people that do not have a lot of time to make plans weeks ahead or that do not want to risk wasting one of their few days off doing nothing. This will be primarely busy parents, college students, or anyone with a demanding career.

Persona 1

  • Name: Robert Jenkins
  • Age: 32
  • Career: Software developer at Google

Background: Robert is a family man. He has been married for 8 years and has 2 children: a 3 year old girl and 6 year old boy. Robert has been able to make a succesful career after graduating from college, but not without sacrifices. His wife is currently going through grad school, so they barely have time off, let alone time to plan for that time off.

Interests: Robert loves his careers, but he loves his family more. He enjoy spending quality time with them, doing things that everyone can enjoy. He loves outdoors and playing ball games with his kids such as soccer, basketball and baseball. He also likes to go on long hikes.

Goals: He wants to have a balanced life between work and family. He wants to have a succesful career without sacrificing mental health or family relationships.

Persona 2

  • Name: Cristina Smith
  • Age: 21
  • Career: Civil Engineering

Background: Cristina is the oldest of 3 children. She came to college when she was 19. At first, she felt college life was fairly easy. She made a lot of friends and went out with them every weekend. This quickly when her third year of college started. Her classes became more difficult and school started taking a toll on her mental health and social life.

Interests: Cristina is not a big fine of outdoor activities, except for swimming. She loves going to her local pools and lakes, swim all day and play games with her friends.

Goals: She wants to keep a 4.0 gpa in order to keep her current scholarship, and also is planning on going to graduate school in the future. Eventually, she wants to work for the government and help build and maintain roads.

Scenarios

  1. A group of freshmen college students are trying to figure out what outdoor activities they can do in a snowy evening in Rexburg.
  2. After finally getting a day off, a busy person makes plans to go fishing. It is super rainy that day, so he wants to come up with a new plan, so that day is not wasted.
  3. A couple makes plans to stay home watching Netflix because they saw in the weather channel there would be a storm that day. Surprinsingly, it is very nice and sunny outside, so they decide to improve outdoor activities instead.
  4. An Elementary teacher decides to plan to take the kids on a trip some time. However, she wants to plan for every possible scenario. Sure, it's Spring, but you never know!
  5. It is sunny outside, but someone in a group of friends hates outdoor activities. They want to come up with some indoor activities they can do in the area.
