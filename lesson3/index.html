Purpose

It is often frustrating for families and friends to make a plan weeks ahead, only for the weather to ruin everything. Our website is designed to help people in Fish Haven, Soda Springs, and Preston, Idaho improvise fun activities no matter what the wheather is like. We aimed at helping them stop worrying them about the future and allow them to simply check the website for today and get a list of fun activities in the area that are safe and fun. Users can also get weather predictions for future days, of course, but if those predictions fail, they come back and check the website for the day for improvised plans.
